I live my life
like a video game.

I believe that everything I design, love, and work for, will unlock an awesome achievement.

I’m constantly working towards the next level, but I’ve made a habit of going back to upgrade my skills and search for hidden treasures.

I like finding new hats to wear more than anything (seriously, you should see my collection!). Sometimes I’m a craftsman, or an accountant, or even a pastry chef.

The design process can get a little crazy. I love what I do for a living.
